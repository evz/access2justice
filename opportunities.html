Opportunities

Thinking about the barriers to finding and making use of reentry resources, beyond the basic goals of the site, we can be thinking of creative solutions to those challenges and barriers:

  • Support being productive while incarcerated
  • Inspire to a get ahead start on finding resources (if less than 5 year to release)
  • Foster hope during incarceration
  • Support utilizing time incarcerated to advance education
  • Take advantage of increased attention from prison counselors prior to release
  • Foster peace of mind about release
  • Support being prepared
  • Timelines
  • Checklists
